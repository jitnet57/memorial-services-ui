Current Pain Points

What can be improved with the existing color scheme

🎨

Indigo feels too "tech/SaaS"

The current #6366f1 indigo primary reads more like a productivity app than a warm memorial service for Filipino families.

🌡️

Low emotional warmth

Cool blue-purple tones lack the compassion and reverence expected from a memorial platform. Needs more warmth.

⚡

Accent color underused

The amber accent (#f59e0b) clashes slightly with indigo. A more harmonious secondary would elevate the design.

🌙

Dark mode could be richer

Current dark bg (#0b0f1a) is very flat. A subtle warm undertone would feel more intimate and premium.
